About us

Overview

  • Urbanization has led to quick and efficient parking being vital.

  • Human-operated parking lots have a lot of inherent flaws.

  • An autonomous parking system could address these issues effectively.

Real world problem

  • Manual systems are highly inefficient and Leads to long queues and waiting times.

  • It is difficult to manage the allocation of parking spaces.

  • Owners cannot easily get an idea of the overall status.

Project objective

This project aims at eliminating the overheadsand inefficiencies associated with manual parking systems in order to provide a comprehensive solution that addresses the concerns of both the consumers as well as the owners of the parking lot.

QuickPark

An autonomous system for assigning and managing parking spots and payments in a car park.
