About Us

We are a group of 3rd year Computer Engineering undergraduates at the University of Peradeniya. QuickPark is a product designed and developed as part of our Third Year Cyber-Physical Systems project aimed at utilizing knowledge of embedded hardware and software, web-based applications and cloud-based distributed systems to solve a real-world practical problem.
